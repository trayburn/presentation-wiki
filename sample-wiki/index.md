# Research Sources Index

A collection of foundational studies and posts surrounding AI adoption, developer cognitive impact, and token-based economics.

## Primary Research Studies

* [BCG & Harvard AI Field Study](/sources/bcg-harvard-study.md) - Landmark randomized field study of 758 consultants showing that AI amplifies good work but makes out-of-boundary work worse.
* [MIT Media Lab Cognitive Debt Study](/sources/mit-cognitive-debt.md) - Research showing that ChatGPT users experience reduced brain activity, lower knowledge retention, and diminished recall.
* [Wharton Cognitive Surrender Research](/sources/wharton-cognitive-surrender.md) - Wharton research showing that high trust in AI leads users to stop constructing answers entirely, resulting in high follow rates for incorrect outputs.

## Industry & Operational Perspectives

* [The Meter Picks the Winners](/sources/devlin-meter-picks-winners.md) - Devlin Liles' post detailing token economics, hidden price increases via tokenizers, and routing discipline with local owned floors.
